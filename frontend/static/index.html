🔗 How Linked Are They?
Correlation: +1 = move together, 0 = independent, −1 = move opposite.
📖 What Does Correlation Mean?
🟢
+1.0 — Move together: When one goes up, so does the other. No diversification benefit.
⚪
0.0 — Independent: Prices move without affecting each other. Holding both reduces overall risk.
🔴
−1.0 — Move opposite: When one falls, the other rises. A perfect hedge (very rare in practice).
💡 Mixing assets with low correlation is the core idea behind portfolio diversification.
